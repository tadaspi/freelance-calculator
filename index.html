Why Most Freelancers Undercharge

If you want $80,000 take-home pay, you need to bill $120,000+ to cover:

  • 15.3% self-employment tax (Social Security + Medicare)
  • ~25% income tax (federal + state)
  • Health insurance ($6,000-$12,000/year)
  • Business expenses (software, equipment, etc.)
  • Non-billable hours (30-40% of your time)

The Formula

Hourly Rate = (Take-Home + Taxes + Expenses) / Billable Hours
